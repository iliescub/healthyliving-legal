Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 4, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to WellFit ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Age range, biological sex, height, weight, activity level, fitness goals, diet preferences, and equipment access
  • Food Log Data: Food names, nutritional information, meal types, timestamps, and optional food photos
  • Weight Tracking: Weight measurements, dates, and optional notes
  • Workout Data: Completed workouts, exercises, sets, reps, and weights (when logged)
  • Water Intake: Daily water consumption logs
  • Apple Health Data (Optional): If you enable Apple Health sync, the app reads active calories, step count, and weight entries from Apple Health, and writes weight, height, nutrition, water intake, and workout data to Apple Health. All syncing happens locally on your device. You can enable or disable individual data categories at any time.

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version

We do not collect analytics, usage data, crash reports, or any other automatically gathered information beyond what is strictly necessary to operate the app.

2.3 Photos, Camera Access, and Barcode Scanning

When you use the food recognition or barcode scanning features, we request access to your device's camera and photo library. Photos are:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never uploaded to our servers
  • Sent to Google Gemini AI when you use the AI photo scanning feature (see Section 5.1)

The app offers two food recognition modes:

  • Cloud AI recognition (Google Gemini) — online: Sends a photo to Google's Gemini API for food identification and nutrition estimation. Used automatically when you are connected to the internet and tap the photo scan button.
  • On-device recognition (Apple Vision) — offline fallback: Uses Apple's Vision framework, which runs entirely on your device. Used automatically when you have no internet connection. No photo or data leaves your device. Offline scans do not count against your daily limit.

No API keys or account credentials are required from you. WellFit uses its own developer API credentials to access these services on your behalf.

When you use the barcode scanner:

  • The camera is used to detect barcodes on food products
  • No images or video are stored — only the decoded barcode number is used
  • The barcode is sent to Open Food Facts to look up product information (see Section 5)

2.4 Apple Health (HealthKit)

If you enable Apple Health integration in Settings, the app accesses HealthKit data on your device. This includes:

  • Data written: Weight, height, nutrition (calories, protein, carbs, fat, fiber, sugar, sodium), water intake, and completed workouts
  • Data read: Active calories burned, step count, and weight entries from external sources (smart scales, other apps)

All HealthKit data is exchanged locally between WellFit and Apple Health on your device. It is never sent to our servers or any third party. You can enable or disable individual read and write categories at any time in Settings → Apple Health.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide personalized nutrition plans based on your profile
  • Generate custom fitness programs tailored to your goals
  • Track your daily nutrition intake and progress toward goals
  • Display analytics and trends about your health data
  • Provide customer support when requested

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

All your health data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Profile information
  • Food logs and nutrition data
  • Weight history
  • Workout logs
  • Photos (stored in app-specific directory)

4.2 No Cloud Backup

We do not maintain any cloud servers or databases. Your data stays on your device and is backed up only through your device's iCloud or iTunes backup (if enabled).

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Services Used by WellFit

WellFit communicates with the following third-party services on your behalf. No personal information (name, email, location) is sent to any of these services.

Google Gemini AI

  • Purpose: AI-powered food recognition and nutrition estimation
  • Data Sent: Food photos (when you use the photo scan feature)
  • When Used: Only when you explicitly tap the photo scan button and are connected to the internet
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

USDA FoodData Central

  • Purpose: Accurate nutritional information from a verified government database
  • Data Sent: Food search queries and food IDs (no personal data)
  • When Used: When you search the USDA database in the Nutrition tab
  • USDA Privacy Policy: https://www.usda.gov/privacy-policy

Open Food Facts

  • Purpose: Product identification and nutritional data from a crowd-sourced food database
  • Data Sent: Food search queries and/or product barcodes (no personal data)
  • When Used: When you search Open Food Facts or scan a barcode
  • Open Food Facts Privacy Policy: https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy

Apple Health (HealthKit)

  • Purpose: Sync health and fitness data between WellFit and Apple Health
  • Data Exchanged: Weight, height, nutrition, water intake, workouts, active calories, steps (all locally on-device)
  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/
  • Your Control: Disabled by default. You must explicitly enable sync in Settings, and can toggle individual data categories on or off at any time.

Apple StoreKit (Subscriptions)

  • Purpose: Processing and managing WellFit Pro subscription payments
  • Data Handled: Subscription purchases are processed entirely by Apple via your Apple ID. WellFit does not receive or store any payment information.
  • Apple Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/privacy/

5.2 No Analytics or Advertising

We do not use third-party analytics services (like Google Analytics or Facebook SDK) and we do not display advertisements. No tracking or advertising SDKs are included in the app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with any third parties, except:

  • When you explicitly use optional third-party features (Gemini AI food scanning, USDA search, Open Food Facts/barcode scanning, Apple Health)
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access Your Data

You can view all your data within the app at any time:

  • Profile: View/edit in Settings → Health Information
  • Food logs: View in Today and Nutrition tabs
  • Weight history: View in Weight tab
  • Workout logs: View in Fitness Plan tab
  • Progress and analytics: View in Status tab

7.2 Export Your Data

Pro subscribers can export all their data in JSON format:

  • Go to Settings → Data Management → Export Data
  • Receive a complete copy of all your health information

7.3 Delete Your Data

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Go to Settings → Data Management to delete data by category
  • Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

7.4 Manage Your Subscription

You can manage or cancel your WellFit Pro subscription at any time through Apple's App Store:

  • Settings → [your Apple ID] → Subscriptions on your device
  • Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

7.5 Opt-Out of Third-Party Services

You can stop using third-party services at any time:

  • Avoid the Photos tab to stop sending food images to Google Gemini
  • Avoid the Open Food Facts and Scan Barcode tabs to stop querying Open Food Facts
  • Turn off Apple Health sync in Settings → Apple Health (or disable individual categories)
  • Use only manual food entry and local features

8. Children's Privacy

WellFit is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us to delete it.

9. International Users

WellFit stores all data locally on your device. If you use features that contact third-party services, your data may be transmitted to servers in other countries (Google servers for Gemini AI, USDA servers in the US, Open Food Facts servers in France). Apple Health data always stays on your device and is never transmitted externally.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via the app store update notes.

12. Legal Compliance

GDPR (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Delete your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Export your data
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Stop using third-party features at any time

CCPA (California Users)

If you are a California resident, you have rights under CCPA:

  • Right to know what data is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of data sales (we do not sell data)
