Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I set up my profile?

When you first open the app, you'll be guided through an onboarding process where you can enter your age, height, weight, activity level, and fitness goals. The app will automatically calculate your personalized nutrition plan.

How are my calorie and macro goals calculated?

Your goals are calculated using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation for Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), adjusted for your activity level and fitness goals (weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain).

Nutrition Tracking

How do I log my meals?

Tap the "Nutrition" tab, then tap the + button to add food. You can use AI-powered food recognition by taking a photo, search the USDA database, or manually enter food items.

Can I customize my nutrition goals?

Yes! After completing onboarding, you can adjust your calorie and macro targets in the Profile section.

Fitness Tracking

How do I log a workout?

Go to the "Fitness" tab to see your workout program. Tap on a workout session to start logging exercises, sets, and reps.

Data & Privacy

Is my data secure?

Yes, all your health data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share your personal health information.

Troubleshooting

The app is running slowly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, restart your device.

AI food recognition isn't working

Check that you've granted camera permissions. For Gemini AI features, ensure you have an active internet connection.
